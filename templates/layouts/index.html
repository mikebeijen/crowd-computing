Welcome!

Thank you for helping us affect the world in a positive way.

The goal of this platform is to make summarizations with either a positive or a negative sentiment of coronavirus related press conferences. We believe this is a useful way to combat negative effects of the coronavirus on our mental wellbeing.

To help out, you will presented with fragments from from YouTube videos. Therefore, make sure that:

  • The video is clearly visible and playable.
  • You can properly hear what is being said.

After watching the video, you will be asked several questions:

  1. How would you rate the sentiment of the video? This question asks you to assign a value to the sentiment expressed by the speaker in the video on a 1 to 7 scale, ranging from very negative to positive.
  2. Did you agree with the content of the video? This questions aks you to assign a value to the extend to which you agree with the content on a 1 to 7 scale, ranging from complete agreement to complete disagreement.
  3. Was the video clear? This questions is asked to detect possible issues with the video such as audio issues, unclear cuts, empty fragment, etc. Tick 'No' if there is an issue; a textbox will pop up asking you to clarify what was wrong.
  4. Whether you have a general comment about the video you saw. This could be anything, so feel free to use this part to give us useful information!
  5. Whether the video was relevant to the coronavirus. Since our goal is to create summarizations of coronavirus related press conferences, we only want to include parts that are actually relevant.
  6. Whether it was easy to identify a single dominant sentiment in the video. If this is not the case, we might need to split our video differently, so we would like to hear this from you.

You will receive $0,10 per rated video, and $0,01 per 10 seconds that a video takes.

Before starting, we will show you some examples.

Good luck!
